abstract: >-
  Private Set Intersection (PSI) allows two parties to compute the intersection of their datasets without revealing anything beyond the result. Homomorphic Encryption (HE)-based PSI protocols provide strong privacy and a non-interactive workflow, involving costly ciphertext-ciphertext multiplications. This paper presents PCPSI, a new HE-based PSI protocol that replaces product-based comparison with a depth-1 plaintext-ciphertext comparison, resulting in up to 8.9x improvement under real cloud environment.
